How to Pay via M-Pesa

  1. Open your M-Pesa menu → Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill
  2. Enter Business Number: YOUR_PAYBILL_NUMBER (register at safaricom.com/business if needed)
  3. Enter Account Number: your email or phone number
  4. Enter Amount: 500 (monthly) or 5000 (annual)
  5. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and confirm
  6. We'll verify payment and activate your account within minutes (check your email/SMS)

Payments are secure and processed via Safaricom Daraja API (coming soon: instant STK Push for one-click payment).
